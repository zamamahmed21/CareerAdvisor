***Career recommendation according to (career) goal***

Required:

Goal (user input)
Career recommendations
Personality Type
Temperament Type


Process:

1)Create tables of Careers of people of each type. Done

2)Create tables of goals.

3)Add attributes to career table:
 lowest_avg_salary, avg_salary, highest_avg_salary, general_description(i.e. in which goal(s) it is useful and how is it going to help a person achieve it)

4)Link them to careers so that we are able to find suitable careers for a goal.

5)Create table of people & link them with careers, temperament & personality types.

6)Match those careers & user's personality type with careers of people present in DB of that personality type.
